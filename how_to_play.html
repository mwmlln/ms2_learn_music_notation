Music Notation Game

How To Play

  1. Before you start the game, look at the alphabets and music notation on the image displayed and remenber which alphabet pairs with the notaion.
  2. Press the start button.
    • The countdown timer will start. You have 60 seconds to play.
    • In the image panel, new image with one note will be displayed.
    • Press the alphabet button for the note of the image.
    • You can press HINT button to display the notation image with alphabets.
    • If your answer is correct, your score will go up.
    • When the time is up, your score is recorded in the Last Score section.
    • Try to get better score than last time!

Enjoy learnig music notaion!
